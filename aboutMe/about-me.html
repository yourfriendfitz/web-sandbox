My Goals:

I have found passion for the design and creation of web content and applications. I hope to channel that passion with others. Success for me means developing content that users will interact with easily, and developing content that can be characterized as fresh, with a pleasing aesthetic. I will seek to establish a reputation for intuitive design, and originality in my work; I hope to be known as a collaborator and team player that treats everyone with courtesy and respect.
